The Spelling Game!

by Tom Nally

Below, find 26 recordings, one for each letter of the alphabet. Your task is to play each recording, then spell the word in the textbox provided. If you spell it correctly, you will be notified Correct! Otherwise, you will be notified Not Correct!

One thing to remember is this: a word that begins with a familiar sound may not be spelled with the letter associated with that sound. For example, xylophone begins with the letter "x", but the sound we hear is "z". To trick the player, xylophone might be placed as a "z" word, because that's the sound we hear!. But unless you provide the correct spelling, you will be provided with a Not Correct! notification.

You may try each word as many times as you like!
